Things People Say

An experiment in hand-lettering that captures spoken or written phrases.

Started in October 2014

The Context

Inspired by two designers (Jessica Walsh and Tim Goodman) and their latest project on Tumblr called QuotesOnShit. They find discarded objects and turn them into art with paint and a catchy idiom.

It gave me the idea to the beautiful phrases people say/write, in a beautiful manner. Ever since I was little, I doodle on paper in different lettering. My "personal dairies" as a kid don't have journal entries; instead they have pages of my first and last names written in 20 or 30 fonts. I used to love it. And now, I want to explore that childhood passion again. Inspired by QuotesOnShit, I want to document things people say or write.

After some research, I purchased a Tombow Brush Tip Pen (recommended by others, and decent price). And I started documenting things people say. Below you'll find the ones I've done so far. I'm aiming to do 4-5 standout phrases weekly and upload in batches. You'll see that, gradually, my lettering gets better.
