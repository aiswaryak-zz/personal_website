My Photography


08/21/2012, Take with Nikon D3100

Photography is important to me. In the past year, I've been in 4 different parts of the world. And as I sit here typing this, I've completed the circle; I'm back where I started.

As temporary as my stay was in each location, it became a kind of home too. Because of the people I've met there. There are some I will see again in a week and others who I may never meet in person again. The moments I remember in Cali or Boston or Chennai -- are conversations, laughter, voices.

My respect for photography has grown the more I travel the world. It takes me back to a time and place and I can look back, and smile, at how I have changed since. It is a reflection of how the people in the photographs changed me too.

Photography is a voluntary track-record of my life. And I'm always practicing to get better at this documentation of my memories.

Enjoy.
