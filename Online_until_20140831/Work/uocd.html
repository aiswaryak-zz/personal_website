User-Centered Design Project

What is UOCD. User-Oriented Collaborative Design (UOCD) is a second-year course offered at Olin College to give students exposure to user-centered design thinking. Students work with one user group throughout the semester.

Get to Know the Users
We met users at computer learning seminars and at help desks of computer retail stores. We identified user needs and values, creating user personas and broad experience journeys, and generating areas of opportunity.

Ideate
We explored the areas of opportunities that we identified. We developed a value map, user scenarios, and gallery sketches.

Develop Solution
We refined the ideas. Lastly, we created mock-ups and prototypes.
