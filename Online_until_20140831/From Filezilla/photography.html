Photography

[04/24/14]: Photographs taken with a Nikon D3100. Some photos are edited in Photoshop, others are untouched. They are taken in several locations: Palo Alto, rural Indian villages, Bangalore, and Chennai. My intention is to capture the people who live in those places - what they do and what they see. I am buying a portrait lens soon, so stay tuned for more pictures.
